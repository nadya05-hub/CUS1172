General Interests

Like I previously mentioned in my general "About" section, I'm into reading, writing, anime, and K-pop.

  • Reading: I mostly read fiction as I feel it lets people truly express themselves creatively, and it's just what catches my interest the best. One of my recent favorites is called Blood On The Tracks.
  • Writing: I've been writing stories since my youth, mostly just for fun and to branch out on my own creative ideas or plots that usually don't get finished before I lose inspiration.
  • Anime: I've been into this practically my whole life, I'd say some of my favorite genres are Shoujo, Slice-of-life, Shounen, and Drama.
  • Gaming: I'm really not a big gamer, but I do enjoy playing games like Stardew Valley, Minecraft, Persona 3-5.

    • Favorite Film
